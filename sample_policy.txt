MEDICAL INSURANCE POLICY

Coverage Details:
- Knee surgeries are covered up to Rs. 1,00,000
- Cardiac procedures covered up to Rs. 2,00,000
- Minimum policy duration: 6 months for coverage
- Age limit: 18-65 years
- Coverage areas: Mumbai, Delhi, Pune, Bangalore

Exclusions:
- Cosmetic surgeries not covered
- Pre-existing conditions require 2-year waiting period
